ABOUT US

LIGHT OF HOPE

Light of Hope is a non-governmental organization dedicated to transforming the lives of children through education. Founded on the belief that every child deserves access to quality learning, the organization focuses on supporting underprivileged and vulnerable communities where educational opportunities are limited. Through initiatives such as providing school supplies, organizing mentorship programs, and offering academic support, Light of Hope aims to remove barriers that prevent children from achieving their full potential. By creating a supportive and inclusive environment, the organization empowers young learners to build confidence, develop skills, and pursue their dreams.

MISSION

The World Association of Non-Governmental Organizations is a global organization whose mission is to serve its member organizations, strengthen and encourage the non-governmental sector as a whole, increase public understanding of the non-governmental community, and provide the mechanism and support needed for NGOs to connect, partner, and multiply their contributions to solve humanity's basic problems.


PATTERNS

Light of Hope works collaboratively and in partnership with UN member states, government agencies, NGOs, international organizations and the private sector worldwide to support them in improving local and global outcomes and to strengthen the non-governmental sector.
